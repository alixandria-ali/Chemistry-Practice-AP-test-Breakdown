📖 Questions

Long Free-Response Questions (10 points each, ~23 minutes)

10 pts Question 1: Ethanol Combustion

Topics: Thermochemistry, stoichiometry, oxidation-reduction, intermolecular forces, equilibrium
Redox reactions, gas laws, heat calculations, hydrogen bonding, Le Chatelier's Principle

10 pts Question 2: Hydrogen Peroxide Decomposition

Topics: Particulate representations, energy diagrams, catalysis, kinetics
Molecular diagrams, activation energy, catalyst effects, reaction rates, balanced equations

10 pts Question 3: Sulfur Trioxide

Topics: Lewis structures, VSEPR theory, thermodynamics, equilibrium
Molecular geometry, Gibbs free energy, Kp calculations, Le Chatelier's Principle

Short Free-Response Questions (4 points each, ~9 minutes)

4 pts Question 4: Hypochlorous Acid Equilibrium

Topics: Acid-base equilibria, titration curves, buffers
Conjugate pairs, weak vs strong acids, buffer requirements

4 pts Question 5: NADH Electrochemistry

Topics: Electrochemical cells, Gibbs free energy, redox reactions
Cell potentials, electron transfer, Nernst equation, ΔG° calculations

4 pts Question 6: Paraffin Wax Phase Changes

Topics: Thermal energy, heat of fusion, particle-level explanations
Endothermic processes, phase transitions, density, molecular spacing

4 pts Question 7: Phenol Red Spectrophotometry

Topics: pH calculations, Beer's Law, dilution, spectroscopy
pH/[H⁺] conversions, calibration curves, absorbance, wavelength effects
