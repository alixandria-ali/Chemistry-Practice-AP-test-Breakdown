## Quick Reference Guides

### Important Formulas
- **Molarity**: M = moles / L
- **Dilution**: M₁V₁ = M₂V₂
- **pH**: pH = -log[H⁺]; [H⁺] = 10⁻ᵖᴴ
- **pH-pOH**: pH + pOH = 14.00
- **Kw**: [H⁺][OH⁻] = 1.0 × 10⁻¹⁴
- **Ideal Gas Law**: PV = nRT
- **Stoichiometry**: moles = mass / molar mass
- **Percent Yield**: (actual / theoretical) × 100

### IMF Strength Ranking
1. Hydrogen bonding (strongest)
2. Dipole-dipole
3. London dispersion (weakest)

### Common Molecular Shapes (4 e⁻ groups)
- 4 bonds, 0 lone pairs → Tetrahedral
- 3 bonds, 1 lone pair → Trigonal pyramidal
- 2 bonds, 2 lone pairs → Bent
