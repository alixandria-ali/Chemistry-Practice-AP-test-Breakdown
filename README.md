# AP Chemistry Practice Test Breakdown

This repository contains a comprehensive breakdown of an AP Chemistry practice test focusing on **Molecular-Level Forces and Solutions** (Units 2-3).

## Structure

Each question includes:
- **Explanation**: Step-by-step approach to solve the problem
- **Unit & Topic**: Specific AP Chemistry curriculum alignment
- **Try It Now**: A similar practice question
- **Check Yourself**: Feedback with approach and correct answer

## Topics Covered

1. **Chemical Bonding** (Unit 2)
   - Pi bonds and orbital overlap
   - Ionic vs. covalent bonding
   - VSEPR theory and molecular geometry
   - Bond polarity and molecular polarity

2. **Intermolecular Forces** (Unit 2)
   - Hydrogen bonding
   - Dipole-dipole interactions
   - London dispersion forces
   - Properties affected by IMFs

3. **Solutions** (Unit 3)
   - Molarity calculations
   - Dilution problems
   - Solution preparation
   - Cohesion and surface tension
   - Factors affecting dissolution rate

4. **Acids and Bases** (Unit 8)
   - pH and pOH calculations
   - Strong vs. weak acids/bases
   - Bronsted-Lowry definitions
   - Acid-base reactions
   - Nomenclature

5. **Stoichiometry** (Unit 4)
   - Mass-to-mass calculations
   - Limiting reagents
   - Percent yield
   - Gas laws and stoichiometry
